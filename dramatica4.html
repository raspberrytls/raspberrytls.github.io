Dramatica Act 4:

Witch and Gingerbread House

Abandoned by their stepmother, Hansel and Gretel find themselves lost in a forest feared to be inhabited by a cannibalistic witch.

The siblings were wandering aimlessly when something appeared before them: a “gingerbread house”—a type of trap made by witches for the purpose of luring in human children...

However, that house turned out to still be under construction—for it had not yet achieved a state of perfection—and the witch Deicy was relentlessly in pursuit of that final result.

The unexpected arrival of the children threw everyone involved in the construction into disarray, and the siblings actually state that they want to help build the gingerbread house—

The theater troupe Dramatica presents Act 4 based on “Hansel and Gretel” from Grimm's Fairy Tales. It tells an original story about a dramatic and eventful encounter between the witch and the children! Please enjoy the “miracle” that they will create.
