Ougonten ／ Chronicle of Dragon's Ascent Chapter 1

Characters: Mitsuru, Mika, Aira

Mika: Huff... Whew... Thank ya kindly, Shiratori-kun. Wanna take a breather here fer now?

Aira: Yeah, let's. I got a little worn out from the surprising amount of luggage.

Mika: Sorry I dragged ya into this.

Mika: I was thinkin' about takin' home my personal belongings from the Handicrafts Club before I graduate, but there ended up bein' way more t' carry than I thought.

Aira: No no, I had some free time too. Actually, it was really amazing to see your treasured works of art, Kagehira-senpai!

Aira: And while we were going back home together, I got to hear plenty of inside stories about Machina.

Aira: As an idol fan, there couldn't be anything better. ♪ I'm happy as can be!

Mika: I'm glad t' hear that. I feel bad that I can't do nothin' to thank ya fer all the help...

Aira: Being able to hear stories about you and Machina is already more than enough thanks for me!

Aira: Even now when I remember it, I'm still overwhelmed by that fantastical worldview...

Mika: Ahaha, don't praise me too much. I might accidentally grow a big head with the way yer talkin' about me.

Aira: No way! As far as I'm concerned, I still haven't said enough about it.

Aira: That's right! The Machina live was great, but the Test World before that was amazing too!

Mika: Huh?! Shi-Shiratori-kun, ya know about the Test World?

Mika: No one could know 'bout it unless they were logged into that virtual world.

Aira: Ah, I didn't see it personally! I saw a video of it.

Aira: Someone had saved a video of what happened in the Test World, and it was posted on social media.

Aira: When I found that video, I was frustrated. I wondered why I couldn't see the heroic figures of Valkyrie with my own two eyes.

Aira: Itsuki-senpai and Kagehira-senpai at that time... Even now when I remember you, I still can't help but sigh and begin to cry.

Aira: The appearance of you two looking down on everyone from the heavens and bringing about the end of the world is undoubtedly divine!

Aira: The highlight was really the simple-minded Kagehira-senpai who made it rain money like it grows on trees while saying, "Art is an explosion~!"

Mika: S-so it's spreading around that much?!

Aira: Yes! I watched that scene so many times that I thought it was going to burn itself into the screen of my phone.

Mika: No, don't look at it! That's where I think I got way too carried away!

Mika: Ugh... Handin' out money freely like that really was too much. I guess this 's a type of punishment.

Aira: No, no, I actually think it was a good thing! Don't you?

Aira: I loved Kagehira-senpai in that scene so much that I couldn't help but share my thoughts on social media.

Aira: The way Kagehira-senpai looked at that time was like glittering money—

Mitsuru: (Whisper) Mika-nii~chan should have gone into this room. I'll stealthily sneak inside and investigate...

Mika: Ah, It's Mitsuru-kun. Welcome home~. What's wrong, why're ya sneakin' around like that?

Mitsuru: Uwah?! Mika-nii~chan was still in front of the door?

Aira: Ah, I'm sorry! Now that you mention it, we've been standing and talking in front of the door this whole time.

Mika: Nnah~, we're in the way fer anyone who wants t' come in from behind. Sorry, Mitsuru-kun. We must'a scared ya.

Mitsuru: I- I'm perfectly fine! I'm going back to my room now, so I hope you guys have fun talking~!

Mika: Huh– Mitsuru-kun?!

Mika: ...I accidentally made 'im leave. Since he came into the room, I thought he had stuff t' do.

Mika: I wonder if I said somethin' that hurt Mitsuru-kun's feelin's?

Aira: Hm~m... I think he just got a bit too surprised, don't you think?

Aira: At any rate, talking while standing like this is no good. We'll do what we did to Tenma-senpai again, scaring anyone who comes into the room.

Mika: Yeah. I still have t' carry this luggage t' my room too.

Aira: That's right... But before anything else, let's take a break and drink some tea first. It won't take that long to carry the luggage.

Aira: Also, I just want to talk with you a bit longer, Kagehira-senpai!

Mika: I see. Well then, let's make some warm tea first. ♪

Mitsuru: Whew... That was dangerous. I almost got found out there by Mika-nii~chan.

Mitsuru: But with this, I was able to learn more about Mika-nii~chan! I'll write it down right away.

Mitsuru: "Mika-nii~chan was a kind god who enjoyed standing while talking with friends."

Mitsuru: Hm~m... Just this much isn't enough~.

Mitsuru: All ri~ght, I'll find out even more about Mika-nii~chan's divine side!
