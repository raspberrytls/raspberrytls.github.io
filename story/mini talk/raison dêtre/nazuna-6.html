Nazuna Nito ／ Good Taste in Choosing Souvenirs 3

Nazuna: Since Izumi-chin gave me something really nice, I want to get him something in return.

Nazuna: I bought green tea bags, ochazuke sets, and boil-in-the-bag side dishes to give to him. I also got seasonings.

Nazuna: I was told it was plain, but he still accepted it. ♪

SELECT: Seasonings...?

Nazuna: Yep. They sell soy sauce and miso overseas, too, but the flavor actually differs a bit. He said that Japanese food is more to his taste.

Nazuna: It's not that big of a deal, but if that's how Izumi-chin can do his best while far away overseas, then I think I made a good choice with my gift. ♪

SELECT: You're very helpful.

Nazuna: No, not at all. I just did some research so that he'd like what I give him, is all~?

Nazuna: I think that I'll do some research regularly, so that I can pick a present using my own taste next time.

SELECT I think you have good taste.

Nazuna: Ahaha, Thanks. Actually, it's a bit different from the kind of taste I was hoping for, but...

Nazuna: If you also get something as a gift to someone living abroad, a good choice would be Japanese food that's lightweight and keeps a long time. ♪
