Kuro Kiryu ／ Peaceful Time 2

Kuro: I've known Itsuki for a long time now, but... It seems like there's always some kind of trouble around him.

Kuro: Well, it's not like I can speak for anyone else, either. Since he's my childhood friend, whenever I hear about things like conversation and rumors about him, I get concerned.

Kuro: Hm? What kind of trouble was I worried about? Well, I could tell you about some stories from our childhood...

SELECT: You both...

Kuro: We had a childhood? Come on, Little Miss. Who do you think we are?

Kuro: There was a time when me and Itsuki both were innocent and simple-minded, even if you can't imagine it now.

SELECT: I'd like to hear any kind of story.

Kuro: I'm kinda lost when told I can share any sort of story, huh. Maybe...

Kuro: Is it alright if I tell you about how when Itsuki was carefully trying to eat a croissant once, he dropped it and became depressed for a while afterwards?

SELECT You have a good relationship.

Kuro: Well, if you think about the old days when we would play games while covered in mud together, our relationship has changed a lot.

Kuro: Still, that undesirable yet unbreakable relationship continues on even now. I think I'd like to have a "good relationship".
