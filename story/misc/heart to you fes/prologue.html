HEART to YOU FES ／ Prologue

Characters: Eichi, Ibara

Eichi: "Welcome to the ES-sponsored idol event, 'HEART to YOU FES'!"

Ibara: "At 'HEART to YOU FES', we have prepared many events that you will be able to enjoy with our idols!"

Ibara: "By participating in the event, 'to YOU HEARTS' are acquired. They are proof of the love and gratitude for you and our own idols, so to speak!"

Ibara: "We would be happy if you could collect many of them. ♪"

Eichi: "You may collect 'to YOU HEARTS' of many different idols, or a lot of 'to YOU HEARTS' of your favorite individual idols..."

Eichi: "At the venue, many idols are looking forward to seeing all of you. I hope that you all enjoy yourselves as much as you can."

Ibara: "Well then, everyone, let's go~!"

Eichi & Ibara: "Marking the start of 'HEART to YOU FES'!"
