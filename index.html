This is a translation blog with a focus on Valkyrie stories and songs. My work is not professional nor am I fluent in Japanese; I do this for fun and personal practice. Some translations are collaborative. Credit is listed on their respective pages.

Contact me on if you have any questions or see any mistakes regarding a translation. Refer to the list below for how you can use my translations. Regardless, always give credit (mention my username or link to this site or my twitter account).

DON'T

  • Do not use my translations to train AI.
  • Do not use my translations to translate into another language. It's very easy for things to get lost in translation this way.
  • Do not repost full translations anywhere. Screenshots are okay.

DO

  • Screenshot my translations for things such as reaction posts. Credit is appreciated for these so that others may find the translation, and it's even better if you quote retweet my original post directly [ I love seeing people's reactions :) ]
  • Use my translations in quote bots as long as there's visible credit (mention my username).
  • Use my translations for videos as long as there's visible credit on screen at all times when the translation appears. Do not, however, use my lyrics translations for videos, as I intend to make (or already have made) videos for them myself.
  • Ask permission to link my translations on public masterlists.
